About us

Come dine with us at Thai Elephant 2.

A family-owned restaurant that gives your taste buds an experience like no other.

  • Perfect for that romantic date night with that special person.
  • Ideal for family reunions and get-togethers.
  • Satisfying those mid-week Pad Thai cravings.

At Thai Elephant 2, we use the freshest ingredients to create a variety of authentic Thai dishes. Our menu caters to every palate, ranging from the classic Pad Thai to the exotic Bird Nest, with each dish skillfully balancing the four main criteria: spicy, sweet, salty, and sour. Whether you're savoring a simple fried rice order or indulging in our signature homemade ice cream, each plate is prepared to its fullest potential. Come and see for yourselves; we'll welcome you with open arms.
